Swedish Glogg

Authentic Swedish spiced wine.

This Swedish glogg recipe is from Great Aunt Freda,

brought from Sweden in the early 1900s.

What you will need

  • 2 cups of water
  • 12 cardamom seeds
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 12 whole cloves
  • 1/2 orange, zested
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup blanched almonds
  • 4 cups Muscatel wine, or orange Muscat
  • 4 cups port wine (or Burgundy)
  • 2 cups brandy
